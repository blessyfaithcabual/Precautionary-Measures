What are
hydrometeorological
hazards?

Hydrometeorological hazards are processes or phenomena of atmospheric, hydrological or oceanographic nature that may cause loss of life, injury or other health impacts, property damages, loss of livelihoods and services, social and economic disruptions, or even environmental damages.

United Nations International Strategy for Disaster Reduction
(UNISDR) Terminology
